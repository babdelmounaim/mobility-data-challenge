Welcome

Join the 2025 Mobility Data Challenge and help us explore, understand, and innovate with urban mobility data. The goal is to analyze anonymized mobile service usage patterns and propose solutions for real-world challenges.

Dataset Description

The dataset covers multiple urban zones with high-resolution spatio-temporal records of mobile activity over 90 days. It includes aggregated service usage metrics across dozens of apps and services.

  • Spatial resolution: 100m x 100m
  • Temporal resolution: 15 minutes
  • Size: 1.8 TB (compressed formats available)
  • Format: CSV, Parquet, NumPy
